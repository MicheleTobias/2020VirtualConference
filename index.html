UC GIS Week 2020

November 17th - 19th

Conference Details

The UC GIS Week committee is proud to announce the first UC-Wide Gis Week. Join us for a 3-day virtual conference which celebrates the GIS work of the University of California faculty, staff, and students. The University of California System is holding an inaugural UC GIS Week on November 17th â€“ 19th, 2020. We will celebrate all things mapping and geospatial. In light of the challenging times, we are coming together virtually to share our work with our community. If you are a researcher, student, industry partner, alumnus, or community mapper, there is space for you to share your accomplishments and inspire others through your mapping.

The cost to attend will be free, please register to attend REGISTER HERE.

Coordinating a UC Wildfire Response - Speaker: Mono Simione

In November of 2019 the Kincade Fire along with PG&E Public Safety Power Shutoff (PSPS) events created a significant challenge for Campus emergency managers and UCOP leadership. The location and maps of campus facilities along with staff, students, and patients became a critical need. This presentation will discuss the progress by a volunteer group to deliver a common operation picture (COP) to deliver information more quickly to campuses and UCOP.
